About

Witch Bot is a Telegram bot for witches. An easy-to-use tool designed to provide accurate and personalized tarot forecasts and lunar calendar information. Using the bot is simple: first, the user initiates a chat with the bot and selects the desired service - tarot forecast or lunar calendar. The Telegram bot for witches is a powerful tool that enables practitioners to access the knowledge and guidance of tarot and lunar energies at their fingertips. With its accurate readings and lunar calendar information, witches can better understand their path and align their magical practice with the natural rhythms of the universe.

Features

  • Lunar Calendar for every day
  • Tarot card for every day
